Biography

Michael Jackson, known as the "King of Pop," was born on August 29, 1958, in Gary, Indiana. He achieved worldwide fame as a member of the Jackson 5 and later as a solo artist. His innovative music videos for songs like "Thriller," "Beat It," and "Billie Jean" transformed the music industry and made him a global icon.
